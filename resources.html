Living Wage Reference Standard Public Consultation - Feedback Survey

In our Living Wage Reference Standard public consultation sessions, we ask stakeholders for feedback concering various aspects, questions, and issues around the development of a Living Wage Reference Standard. If you want to contribute your feedback, please use this survey to let us know what you think: Living Wage Reference Standard Public Consultation - Feedback Survey.
All comments and polling answers will be anonymous.​ All comments will be posted on the WageMap website after the consultations are included as well as a response on how they have been integrated or why WageMap might not integrate the point.​
