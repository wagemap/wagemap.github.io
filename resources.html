Resources related to the second round of public consultations for the Living Wage Reference Standard

Round 2 Public Consultation Update: October 2025

Many thanks to everyone who participated in Round 2 of our Public Consultations. We are currently reviewing all the feedback to finalise the Living Wage Reference Standard, which will be launched on 26 th November 2025, more information: Launch Event Notice.

Participation & Reach: Survey
We conducted a survey to get detailed feedback on each element of the Living Wage Reference Standard. This survey is now closed but you can view it here: Round 2 Consultation Survey Living Wage Reference Standard.pdf.

We received 68 responses

  • Representing 61 organizations across corporate, NGO, union, research, and government sectors
  • We received responses from organisations representing 190 countries
  • 31% of engagements with the survey came from individuals based in the Global South
Participation & Reach: Focus Groups & 121s
We held focus group sessions, group and individual calls, and also received written feedback to gather perspectives and feedback from key stakeholders, such as unions, local living wage networks and leaders in area of living wages.

  • 41 individuals participated in one of WageMap's fifteen Focus Group consultations run over Summer-Fall 2025 for Round 2.
  • 63% of focus group participants were based in the Global South, and 71% represented an underrepresented stakeholder group.
Next Steps
We will share the final version of the Living Wage Reference Standard and our response to feedback from the consultations once it is ready. You can find out more information about the launch here: Launch Event Notice.

Information on Round 2 Public Consultation & How to Participate

WageMap is referencing the ISEAL Code of Good Practice for Sustainability Systems throughout the process of developing the Living Wage Reference Standard. It is committed to an inclusive and transparent review process and is seeking input from all stakeholders impacted by the implementation of the Living Wage Reference Standard. We ran our first round of public consultations in Fall 2024 and we are running our second round of public consultations in Summer 2025. Living Wage Reference Standard Public Consultation - Feedback Survey (CLOSED)

In our Living Wage Reference Standard public consultation sessions, we ask stakeholders for feedback concering various aspects, questions, and issues around the development of a Living Wage Reference Standard. If you want to contribute your feedback, please use this survey to let us know what you think: Living Wage Reference Standard Public Consultation - Feedback Survey.
