Living Wage Knowledge Hub

The Living Wage Knowledge Hub aims to strengthen support for businesses implementing global living wages. It will bring together expertise and resources from the global living wage community, create a space for businesses to share implementation experience and explore opportunities for joint action.

We are building a community of learning and support employers to advocate for living wages and share their knowledge to drive progress on living wages.

If you are interested in finding out more, sharing your learnings at one of our Knowledge Hub sessions or exploring opportunities for joint action, then complete our survey to help us shape the Knowledge Hub or contact lauren.wetherilt@livingwage.org.uk

Video Recording of the first WageMap Knowledge Hub Session

View the recording of the first Knowledge Hub session on demonstrating the business case for paying living wages globally.

We heard from you in our survey that the business case was the was one of the highest priority learning topics. This session covered:

  • Why living wages are an important part of the wider sustainability agenda, considering risks and human rights.
  • Framing living wage as an investment rather than a cost: insights on benefits for the worker, business, investor and economy.
  • How to measure the impact linked to established business benefits.
  • Insights from an employer panel on how to get buy-in from senior leadership and monitoring benefits.
