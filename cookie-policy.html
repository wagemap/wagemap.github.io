Introduction

Welcome to WageMap Initiative ("we", "our", "us"). This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website wagemap.org (the "Site"). By using the Site, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit a website. Cookies help to enhance your browsing experience by remembering your preferences and visits.

Types of cookies we use

1. Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how our visitors interact with the Site. These cookies collect information anonymously and generate reports on website activity. We use this information to improve our Site and to ensure it meets the needs of our users.

Cookies used on this Site

1. Google Analytics

  • Purpose: To analyze the use of our Site and compile reports on user activity.
  • Data collected: Anonymized information such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on the Site, and other relevant metrics.
  • How to opt-out: You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on, available at Google Analytics Opt-out.

2. Cookie Consent

  • Purpose: To store your consent to the use of cookies for up to one month.
  • Data collected: Your consent to the use of cookies on our site. This prevents the cookie notification banner from appearing every time you visit the Site after consenting to the use of cookies.
  • How to opt-out: You can opt out by clicking "decline" on the cookie notification banner.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways. Most browsers allow you to accept or reject cookies, delete cookies, and set preferences for certain websites.

  • Browser settings: You can usually find cookie settings in the "options" or "preferences" menu of your browser.
  • Opt-out tools: To opt out of analytics cookies, you can use tools such as the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on mentioned above.

Your Consent

By using the Site, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this Cookie Policy. If you do not agree to the use of these cookies, please disable them using the instructions above or refrain from using the Site.

Changes to this Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or relevant laws. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for the latest information on our cookie practices.
